Available Hypervisors and Platforms

For production deployment, the officially supported hypervisor platform is VMware ESXi (5.x / 6.x). The “Installation” part of this guide focuses on ESXi deployment. Once installed, the configuration guidelines apply also to other types of deployment.

For PoC or personal testing only, VM image is also reported to work out-of-the-box on other VMware products (e.g. Fusion, Player, Workstation).

VM can also be installed on Virtualbox, and the IllumOS version can be installed on KVM, but installation must be performed manually on these platforms and might require some fine-tuning.

Other types of deployment: Cloudian HF Appliances or bare-metal deployments are out of the scope of this document.
